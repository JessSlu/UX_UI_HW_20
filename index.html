Adopt A Golden Birmingham

Adopt-a-Golden in Birmingham, AL was established to find loving homes for Golden Retrievers and Golden Retriever Mixes in the Birmingham area.

A small group a volunteers dedicate their time and energy to find safe and loving homes for Golden Retrievers, providing medical and safety before finding the perfect match for the dogs they take under their care.

No Golden Retriever is turned away, regardless of age or medical needs.
